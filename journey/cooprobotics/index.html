During my 2024-2025 senior capstone project in the electrical and computer engineering department, I worked with a team to build an end to end pipeline for multiple robot manipulators working together to execute a non-planar 3D printing process in ROS2. To see our progress, check out the video and poster.

I researched the algorithms to resolve conflicts between the two robots. It is an extremely fascinating problem and has so many connections to other branches of math and computer science I never expected. It has lots of applications in the manufacturing world, but I am particularly excited by its use cases in 3D printing.

While I am no longer working on this project in the capacity as a student, I hope to explore these challenges with my own free time. I’ll be sharing my insights on my website and invite you to reach out if you’re interested in collaborating!
