I had the idea for this as I was walking alongside my friends who always tease how annoying it is to bring my bike around campus. Never tease an engineer because now I’ll have the last laugh!

How hilarious would it be if I could have my bike follow me so I can walk with my non-bike friends without worry. Or what if my bike could pick me up like an Uber?

This is a project I will be working on for the summer of 2023. I’m really excited because it’s a super interesting physics and controls system problem. Plus the end result is beyond amazing.
