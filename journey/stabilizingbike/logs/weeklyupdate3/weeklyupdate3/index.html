Phase 1 Week 1

Initializing plan… begin execution

October 13, 2024

mechanical robotics electronics

I took a very long hiatus away from this project. I had some personal issues that I had to sort out before I could really commit myself to this project. I’m sure I will write about it another time.

I did not have a good approach when I initially started this project. I learned that I struggle to make progress without deadlines. And when it comes to personal projects, nobody will set the deadline unless I do… not exactly a recipe for success.

As much as I hate how overused this acronym is, SMART goals have a point. SMART stands for: Specific, Measureable, Atainable, Relevant, Time bound. My previous “plan” fails almost all the criteria.

I generally know what I need to accomplish but I was operating with a completely unrestricted timeframe. That allowed me to dilly dally and become demoralized at the lack of progress I was making. It is extremely important for me to have a meaningful deadline and not something I made it. I have always wanted to go to the annual Maker Faire or Open Sauce. Coincidentally, I will be in the Bay Area for an internship this summer when Open Sauce is going on. So by Open Sauce 2025, I will have finished my project and hopefully be able to show it off at the event.

I’ve broken up the project into three different phases, each approximately 11 weeks long. Phase 1 will be working towards building a first working prototype while Phase 2 will be polishing and removing the jank. Phase 3 at the moment is entirely allocated for unexpected delays at the moment. But as the weeks progress, it will hopefully become more clear what Phase 3 will encompass.
