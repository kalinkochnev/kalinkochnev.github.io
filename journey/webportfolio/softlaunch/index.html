Soft launch of the site!

The site might be rough on the edges, but bear with me

February 2, 2023

web

Hi and thank you for taking a look at my site! It’s currently under construction, and will be for quite a while. There might be content missing here and there, but I hope it’s not too distracting. I promise I’ll get there!

As of right now, here are my priorities for future updates:

  1. Write and publish all the content I have stocked up on my journeys and blog
  2. Add a nice footer
  3. Make the site mobile friendly
  4. Clean and refactor styling
  5. Refactor the Hugo theme to be more modular
  6. Publish the theme for others to use!
  7. SEO optimization

This may take me a few months since I am just itching to get started on some of my other projects. If you have any feedback I would really appreciate it! Feel free to send me an email
